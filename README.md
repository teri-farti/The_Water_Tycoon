# Gameplay Mechanics

  Water Extractors  
    Workers who collect water from natural sources.
    They feature custom animations, behavior, and a full work cycle.

  Filters & Purification Stations  
    Players improve water quality to increase profit and efficiency.

  Couriers  
    Deliver purified water to the market.
    They follow routes and use custom models and animations.

  Economic System  
    Players earn money by selling water and expanding their infrastructure.

  Progression & Upgrades  
    Improve workers, filters, delivery speed, and extraction volume.
